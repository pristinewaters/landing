About Us

At Pristine Waters, we’re not just a fly-fishing outfitter — we’re storytellers of Patagonia’s rivers, guardians of its silence, and companions on journeys that awaken something deeper.

Born and raised in the southernmost region of the world, our team of passionate anglers and naturalists crafts bespoke fly-fishing adventures that blend world-class angling with the spirit of wild Patagonia.

Each experience combines expert guiding, premium equipment, gourmet field cuisine, and a deep respect for nature through sustainable practices and responsible catch & release.

Whether you seek the thrill of a sea-run brown trout on the Río Grande, the serenity of a spring creek in Tierra del Fuego, or the majesty of Torres del Paine’s glacial waters — every cast becomes a story, every river a memory, and every journey a lesson written by Patagonia itself.

Fishing Programs

Our excursions celebrate the extraordinary diversity of southern Chile — from windswept pampas and turquoise lagoons to ancient forests and glacial rivers. Each outing blends adventure, serenity, and discovery.

Whether for a day or a week, every program is a journey through landscapes that redefine the meaning of “remote.” Here, time slows, instincts sharpen, and nature rewards those who listen.
