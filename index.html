About Pristine Waters

At Pristine Waters, we believe that fly fishing is more than a sport — it’s a connection to nature, culture, and conservation. Based in Punta Arenas, Chile, we guide anglers through some of the most remote and untouched waters, combining professional expertise with deep respect for the environment.

Destinations — Magallanes & Chilean Patagonia

Punta Arenas

Ideal for day trips and short adventures. Explore rivers and spring creeks near the southernmost city on the continent.

Tierra del Fuego

Legendary for its sea-run brown trout. Experience multi-day expeditions across pristine, wind-swept landscapes.

Torres del Paine

Combine fly fishing with the breathtaking beauty of Patagonia’s most iconic national park — an unforgettable mix of wilderness and wonder.
