Fly Fishing in Punta Arenas

Where silence, clarity, and the southern wind meet.

South of Punta Arenas, the landscape opens into endless steppe — a land where the sky dominates and the rivers carve quiet lines across the earth...

The spring creeks near Punta Arenas flow like veins of glass through low vegetation and golden grass...

On the Río San Juan, the breeze carries the scent of salt and peat...

The Río del Medio offers intimate water — a setting for those who appreciate stealth and observation...

At Laguna Parrillar, reflections blend with clouds and mountain ridges...

Distances from Punta Arenas: between 50 and 90 km depending on the river or lagoon selected.

Included: Bilingual guide, 4×4 transport, premium gear, waders, flies, and gourmet river lunch.

Each outing is designed not as a rush, but as an encounter — a day shaped by light, water, and the subtle grace of southern Patagonia...
