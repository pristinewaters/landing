Fly Fishing in Punta Arenas

Where silence, clarity, and the southern wind meet.

South of Punta Arenas, the landscape opens into endless steppe — a land where the sky dominates and the rivers carve quiet lines across the earth. Here, the experience of fly fishing becomes something deeper than the pursuit of fish; it becomes a return to simplicity and presence.

The spring creeks near Punta Arenas flow like veins of glass through low vegetation and golden grass. Their clarity challenges perception — every shadow, every ripple tells a story. Patience and delicacy become the true tools of the angler.

On the Río San Juan, the breeze carries the scent of salt and peat. The river meanders through wide valleys, its calm stretches perfect for sight fishing brown trout. It’s a place where time slows, where each cast feels like a quiet dialogue with the wind.
